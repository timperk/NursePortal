Updates from your VP of Nursing

Clinical

Leaders

Strategic oversight of clinical processes, programs and education that improve patient quality outcomes and adhere to regulatory requirements

Front Line Managers

Manage end to end clinical processes, programs and direct patient care. Track metrics and support development of policies and procedures

Individual Contributors

Deploy/Oversee clinical training, survey readiness, and policies & procedures to patient care facilities (dialysis facility, hospital, in home, in clinic)

Village (Atlas)

Leaders

Management and oversight of risk. Serves as clinical P&P resource, ensures clinical programs meet federal, state, and local regulations

Front Line Managers

Project/Program management of enterprise wide initiatives, Provides support of strategic initiatives through consulting and developing training

Individual Contributors

Corporate specialists supporting various enterprise wide initiatives (including IT, People Services)

Operations

Leaders

Management and oversight of division operations. Provides strategic and tactical leadership, counsel and direction to field managers. Ensures operations performance is aligned with mission and values. Budget and P&L accountability

Front Line Managers

Manage operations of facilities that provide direct patient care. Provide direction and counseling to teammates, monitor workflow functions, outcomes, customer satisfaction and quality measures of the facility

Individual Contributors

Nurses educating patients or caring for patients in a facility (dialysis facility, hospital, in home, in clinic) or telephonically

Direct Patient Care

This is a foundational path where nurses can provide patient care and grow into a clinical expert across multiple settings (modalities, hospitals, facilities, clinics etc.) This path provides opportunities for nurses to increase the breadth and depth of their experience in patient care
